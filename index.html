Visualizing Student-AI Writing Collaboration

Team Member: Momin Siddiqui, Olivia Hu, Jinkua Liu,

Introduction

The rise of AI technologies like ChatGPT has sparked concerns among educators and parents about their role in student learning, particularly the risk of students bypassing essential writing processes. While restricting AI use is impractical, integrating these tools within monitored educational environments presents an opportunity to enhance learning. To support this, we are interested in investigating if we can showcase the productive struggle made by students while writing with AI using salient visual encodings. Specifically, we will analyze the following key metrics: 1) student-AI contribution ratio; 2) writing fluency enabled by AI; 3) engagement with ai feedback.
